My Interests

Interests:

Folktales, in general, are my favorite books to read. They often carry profound messages and reflections on human nature, passed down through generations. The storytelling traditions embedded in these tales provide a window into different cultures and their values. One of my current fascinations is with Japanese folklore, particularly the yokai. These mythical creatures and spirits from Japanese tradition are captivating due to their rich and varied lore. Each yokai has its own unique story and characteristics, which makes exploring them a deeply intriguing experience.

Music theory is another area that interests me. Understanding the principles behind music composition enhances my appreciation of various musical styles and helps me connect with the emotional depth of different pieces. I really enjoy exploring various scores from movies, anime, and videogames. Some of my favorite composers are: Hans Zimmer, Kevin Penkin, and Yu Peng Chen. Music theory offers me endless avenues for exploration and enjoyment.

Baking, my third interest, is a creative outlet that combines science and art. Is what I would say, but the truth is that I just love sweet treats. Baking also allows me to express my creativity and share delightful treats with friends and family.
